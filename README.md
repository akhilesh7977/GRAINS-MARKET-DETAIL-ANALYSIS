# 🌾 Grain Arrival Trends and Rate of MAPMC Market Analysis

> An interactive **Power BI Dashboard** analyzing grain arrival volumes and market rates across APMC (Agricultural Produce Market Committee) — covering data from **2024 to 2026**.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

This report provides a comprehensive view of grain market trends at **MAPMC (Mumbai Agricultural Produce Market Committee)**, enabling traders, analysts, and stakeholders to monitor price movements and arrival quantities with ease.

## 📈 Visuals & Charts

### 1. 📦 Monthly Grain Arrivals (Tonne)
- Bar chart showing **month-wise grain arrivals** from Jan 2024 to March 2026
- Peak arrivals observed at **100K tonnes** (March 2024)
- Tracks seasonal trends across all years

### 2. 💰 Average Rate (/KG) in APMC
- Line chart comparing **average price per kg** for multiple grain varieties
- Covers the full period from **Jan 2024 to March 2026**
- Helps identify price fluctuations and market trends

### 3. 🥧 Total Grain Arrivals (Tonne) in APMC
- Pie chart showing **grain-wise share** of total arrivals
- **Top grains by volume:**
  - 🥇 Rice-Tandul — 39.59% (699.58K T)
  - 🥈 Harbhara — 14.99% (264.86K T)
  - 🥉 Gahu — 6.5% (114.78K T)

### 4. 📉 Grain Arrival Quantity (Tonne) By Months
- Multi-line area chart tracking **month-by-month arrival** by grain type
- Compare performance of Bajari, Chavali, Gahu, Harbhara & more

## 🏢 About MAPMC

**MAPMC (Mumbai Agricultural Produce Market Committee)** is one of India's largest agricultural markets, regulating the trade of food grains, vegetables, and other produce in the Mumbai region. This dashboard helps monitor grain flow and pricing at APMC centers.
